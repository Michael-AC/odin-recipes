Miso Soup

Image and recipe credit: https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/13107/miso-soup/

Description

Miso soup is a flavourful soup of Japanese origin.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of water
  • 2 teaspoons of dashi (だし) powder
  • 3 tablespoons of miso paste
  • 8 ounces of diced tofu
  • 2 green onions sliced in 1/2 inch pieces

Steps

  1. Combine water and dashi (だし) powder over medium-high heat and bring to a boil.
  2. Reeduce heat to medium and whisk in the miso paste.
  3. Stir in the tofu.
  4. Add the green onions.
  5. Let simmer for 2-3 minutes.
