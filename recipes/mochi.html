Mochi

Image and recipe credit: https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/193307/easy-mochi/

Description

Mochi (もち) is a Japanese sweet cake made with glutinous rice flour.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of sweetened red bean paste
  • 1 cup of glutinous rice flour
  • 1 teaspoon of green tea powder
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1/2 cup of cornstarch

Steps

  1. Wrap the red bean paste in aluminum foil and place it in the freezer until it's solid.
  2. Mix the glutinous rice flour and green tea powder in a microwave-safe bowl.
  3. Stir in the water, then stir in the sugar. Mix until it's smooth.
  4. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and microwave it for 3 minutes and 30 seconds.
  5. Remove red bean paste from freezer and divide into 8 equal balls.
  6. Create a work surface with the cornstarch. Roll 2 tablespoons of the hot rice flour mixture into a ball. Flatten the ball and place one ball of frozen red bean paste in the centre. Pinch and press the dough around the bean paste.
  7. Repeat the prior step to create the remaining mochi.
