Oyakodon

Image and recipe credit: https://www.justonecookbook.com/oyakodon/

Description

Oyakodon (親子丼) is a Japanese chicken and egg rice bowl.

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Sake
  • Onion
  • Eggs
  • Mitsuba (三つ葉)
  • Steamed rice
  • Seasonings: Dashi (だし), soy sauce and sugar

Steps

  1. Prepare the ingredients (cut the onions and chicken, and crack the eggs).
  2. Cook the onions in the seasoning mixture.
  3. Add the chicken and cook until no longer pink.
  4. Drizzle the egg mixture.
  5. Garnish with the mitsuba.
  6. Once finished, spoon the dish over the bowl of steamed rice.
